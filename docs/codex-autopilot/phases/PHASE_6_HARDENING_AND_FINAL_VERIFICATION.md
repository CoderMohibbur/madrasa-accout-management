# PHASE 6 — HARDENING AND FINAL VERIFICATION

## Objective

Perform final regression hardening, restrict or retire unsafe legacy paths, verify compatibility, and prepare deployment readiness.

## Scope

- final regression validation
- controlled retirement or lock-down of conflicting legacy write paths
- cleanup of obsolete screens only after compatibility verification
- final authorization and receipt traceability verification

## Hard Restrictions

- do not remove legacy paths until regression proof exists
- do not declare completion without final verification state = `passed`
