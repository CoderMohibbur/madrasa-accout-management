# Decisions

- The product baseline will be a light-first institutional design system: calm, trustworthy, editorial on public surfaces, and operationally clear on logged-in surfaces.
- Guardian, donor, management, auth, public, and future multi-role surfaces must share one product family, with role accents limited to small markers rather than separate full-screen themes.
- The strongest current portal pattern to preserve is the information architecture of title plus description plus summary cards plus scoped panels, but it must be normalized into shared shell and component primitives.
- Stock Breeze auth styling, the current public welcome-page styling, and page-local token definitions are not the target standard and should be replaced in later implementation.
- The minimum shared foundation must include tokenized colors, spacing, typography, radii, shadows, shared shells, buttons, fields, alerts, cards, tables, empty states, loading states, and no-access states before fragmented feature-page UI work continues.
- Prompt-19 remains preserved: later admission CTA design must stay external-only and limited to approved public and guardian-informational placements, not auth or current protected guardian pages.
