# Decisions

- Freeze the distinction between role membership and portal eligibility: roles indicate potential domains, while portal entry remains derived from profile lifecycle and domain-specific authorization.
- Freeze donor portal access as donor-domain-only and independent from guardian linkage or student-owned data.
- Freeze guardian informational and guardian protected access as separate boundaries with different eligibility thresholds.
- Freeze guardian linkage as the decisive additional requirement for protected student/invoice/payment access beyond guardian role or guardian profile presence.
- Freeze multi-role home behavior as eligibility-driven rather than hard-coded guardian-first role ordering.
