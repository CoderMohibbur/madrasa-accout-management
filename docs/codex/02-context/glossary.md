# Glossary

- **Guest donor**: a donor who can donate without prior registration.
- **Identified donor**: a donor whose donation is associated with usable identity/contact data.
- **Anonymous-display donor**: a donor whose donation may be recorded internally but displayed without exposing identity.
- **Guardian informational portal**: a light portal surface for non-sensitive information.
- **Guardian protected portal**: a linked, authorized surface for student-owned sensitive data.
- **Portal eligibility**: the state that determines whether a user may use a specific portal surface.
- **Linkage**: the verified/authorized association between a guardian and student-owned data.
