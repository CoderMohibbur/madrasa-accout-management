# Guardian Informational Portal Requirements

- Guardian may log in without verification.
- Unverified or unlinked guardian may view non-sensitive institution and panel information.
- Guardian may view admission-related information.
- Protected academic, invoice, receipt, and payment-sensitive areas must remain linkage- and authorization-controlled.
- The system must clearly separate:
  - public institution information
  - public admission information
  - authenticated light guardian informational portal
  - linked guardian protected portal
