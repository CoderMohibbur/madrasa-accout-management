# Prompt 03 Business Rule Freeze

Approved baseline decisions from prompt-03:

- Keep a single authenticated account model that may hold donor and guardian roles simultaneously.
- Allow donor registration, login, and donation without mandatory verification, while preserving payment-domain safety and traceability.
- Allow unverified or unlinked guardians to access only a light informational portal until linkage and authorization are satisfied.
- Treat guest donation as mandatory target behavior with optional human identity fields but mandatory operational traceability.
- Keep admission scope informational only with an external application handoff.
- Treat Google sign-in and dual email/phone verification as later-phase required capabilities, not current-state assumptions.
