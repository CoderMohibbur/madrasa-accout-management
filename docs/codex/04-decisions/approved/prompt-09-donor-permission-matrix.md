# Prompt 09 Donor Permission Matrix

Approved baseline decisions from prompt-09:

- Donor payment ability and donor portal eligibility are separate permissions.
- Donor login and donor donation are not universally dependent on email or phone verification; approval and lifecycle remain separate gates.
- Guest donation remains allowed without prior registration and with optional human identity fields, while operational traceability stays mandatory.
- Transaction-specific receipt or payment-status access is a narrower permission than full donor portal history access.
- `guest donor`, `identified donor`, and `anonymous-display donor` are the canonical donor terms; `hidden donor` is only a legacy synonym for `anonymous-display donor`.
- Recurring donation and saved payment methods remain out of the initial safe donor rollout.
- Legacy `transactions` do not yet define the approved donor live-payment finalization model; prompt-10 must establish the dedicated donor payable path.
