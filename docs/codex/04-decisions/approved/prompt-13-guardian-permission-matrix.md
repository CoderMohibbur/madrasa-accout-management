# Prompt 13 Guardian Permission Matrix

Approved baseline decisions from prompt-13:

- Guardian informational access and guardian protected access are separate approved boundaries.
- Guardian login and guardian informational access must not depend on universal email or phone verification.
- Registration or guardian intent capture may create only an unlinked informational-state guardian record; it must not create protected access.
- Role membership expresses guardian-domain potential only; it is not the final guardian portal gate.
- Linked students, student details, invoices, receipts, payment history, and invoice payment actions remain linkage-controlled or authorization-controlled surfaces.
- The smallest safe guardian rollout is: one shared account model, guardian intent capture, a separate authenticated informational portal, and a separately gated protected portal for linked guardians only.
- The current repository is narrower than the approved target because the live `/guardian` routes are protected-only and there is no separate informational guardian portal yet.
- Prompt-12 donor slice-planning decisions remain preserved and are not reopened by guardian analysis.
